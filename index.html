About

IBeTo (Innovations for a Better Tomorrow) is the flagship event of Excel 2019, by the credence that technology holds the key to unlocking societyâ€™s most unnerving challenges. The 7th edition of IBeTo seeks to replicate and augment the immense responses received from numerous colleges all over India, bringing forward some of the most spectacular innovations that benefit society. The brightest of these ideas are recognized and provided mentorship to bring about innovative end products every year. Futhermore, the winner is awarded a cash prize of Rs 1 lakh. IBeTo thus aspires to unite social relevance and technical innovation, to the pioneer and the populace.

Rewards

IBeTo 2019 offers an added advantage by associating with Kerala Startup Mission(KSUM) so as to seek their support to transform the products developed into a commercial commodity through startups.

  • The best projects chosen by the judges will be awarded with a cash prize worth INR 1,00,000/- from the organisers.
  • Projects chosen to win the idea-grant by KSUM will receive an additional prize worth Rs 2,00,000.
  • Projects chosen to win the prototype-grant by KSUM will receive an additional prize worth Rs 7,00,000 after registering as a startup.
  • Certificate of Participation will be awarded, signed from the concerned authorities.
  • As per KTU regulations, socially relevant projects should be given 50 activity points.
